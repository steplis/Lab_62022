About Lisa's Dog Grooming

Background

Growing up, I have always had a special bond with my family's dogs.
After graduating high school, I realized that I could make a living out of
caring for man's best friend!

Our Salon

In June of 2020, I was able to open my first Doggie Salon! it has been a dream come true, and I have enjoyed every second of it.

Moving Forward

Looking forward to the future, I hope to keep improving the lives of all my clients and their furry friends far into the future, one haircut at a time!
