Welcome to Lisa's Dog Grooming!

Here at Lisa's Dog Grooming, we treat your pet like family.
With skilled hands and hearts full of love, we will ensure that your pet is taken care of.
We offer the following services, and can tailor anything to you or your pet's needs.
  • Treat your pet with a pet spa
  • Trim your pet's perfect fur with a shampoo, blow dry, and conditioning treatment
  • Each treatment comes with a nail trim, and ear cleaning
  • Each dog leaves with a baggie of treats, and a toy of their choice!
